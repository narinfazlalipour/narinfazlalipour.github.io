6. The Storytelling Engine — How to Tell It

🎯 Story Objective: 2.1K → 10K Followers, 5%+ Engagement, Founder Authority Cemented

The engine runs on a single principle: every post should answer one of three questions. What does Sean believe? What has Sean learned? What is Sean building? If a post answers one of those, it grows an audience. If it doesn't — stage photos, panel recaps, product announcements — it's noise.

1

Face-to-Camera Reels — 3×/week (The Trust Format)

A founder speaking directly to camera for 60–90 seconds is the highest-trust format on Instagram. Three modes to rotate:

"What I Believe" (1×/week): One contrarian take on health, entrepreneurship, or building. "I've spent 15 years in healthcare. Here's the one thing everyone gets wrong."
"What I Learned" (1×/week): One specific lesson from the journey. "When we sold our first company, I thought we'd made it. Here's what actually happened."
"Behind the Build" (1×/week): Raw BTS of Verséa. The lab, the team, the hard days. "This is what building a NASA-science health company actually looks like."

Rules: hook in first 1.5 seconds. Text on screen for entire video. End with a question ("What would you do?" "Have you experienced this?").

2

Founder Frameworks Carousels — 1×/week (The Save Format)

Carousels get 2–3× the reach of static posts and drive saves — Instagram's strongest algorithmic signal. Template: Slide 1 = bold claim → Slides 2–7 = one framework element → Slide 8 = "Save · Share with a founder who needs this."

8 ready frameworks: "5 lessons from launching Red Bull USA" · "The 3 biggest mistakes I made selling 8 companies" · "What 110 countries taught me about building" · "How to pitch NASA-level science to investors" · "The founder's guide to conference networking" · "Why I went from energy drinks to cellular energy" · "4 things I wish I knew before starting Verséa" · "How to build a brand that survives pivots"

3

Stage Clip Reels — 1×/week (The Credibility Format)

Every stage appearance at Biohacking Summit, A4M, Dave Asprey conference should produce 2–3 Reels. Hook with his most provocative line → 45–60s of the best moment → End card: "Full talk in bio." Stage content borrows the credibility of the venue. Audiences trust curated highlights more than original posts because the conference already vetted him.

4

The Founder Arc — Monthly Narrative Series

The Red Bull → 8 exits → Verséa → mescreen arc is a four-part mini-series. One Reel per week for one month:
Chapter 1: "How I went from launching energy drinks to studying cellular energy"
Chapter 2: "What selling 8 companies taught me about knowing when to hold"
Chapter 3: "The moment I realized healthcare was the biggest problem worth solving"
Chapter 4: "Why I wake up every day to build the future of mitochondrial testing"

Audiences binge story arcs. This is the highest-retention content format on the platform.

5

Fix the Signal — Immediate

Following ~2,000 accounts with ~2,100 followers undermines every story before it's read. Unfollow down to <100 — keep close friends, collaborators, and accounts you genuinely consume. A founder at 2,100/85 reads as curated authority. Same numbers. Different signal entirely.
