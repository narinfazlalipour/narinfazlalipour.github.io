  3. Product-market clarity — "Why you're tired" is a universal hook; mitochondrial dysfunction connects to energy, aging, brain fog
  4. "Comment-to-engage" tactics — Posts like "Comment REPORT and I'll send you a sample report" generate conversational engagement
  5. 17 distinct profiles — "We've developed 17 distinct mitochondrial profiles" is fantastic serializable content waiting to be deployed
  6. Leadership credibility — PhDs, double-board-certified CMO, founder with multiple exits — the faces behind this account have real authority
  7. 3–4 week results timeline — Unboxing → waiting → results → interpretation = built-in content arc with natural re-engagement

What's Missing

  1. No consistent posting cadence — Algorithm punishes irregular accounts; 4K followers with single-digit likes on multiple posts signals suppressed reach

  3. No customer transformation stories — "My mescore was 42, I changed X, now it's 78" — the most powerful content format is entirely absent

  6. No Stories strategy — Polls, Q&A boxes, and behind-the-scenes are missing; Stories build the daily touchpoint
