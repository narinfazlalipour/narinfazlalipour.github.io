The Approach

Conversational, person-on-the-street. No clipboard energy. I act as both interviewer and someone who genuinely knows the science — because I do. Questions are designed to open people up, not box them in, and mescreen™ is introduced naturally in conversation rather than as a hard pitch. The science sells itself when explained by someone who understands it.

  1. "When you hear the word mitochondria, what comes to mind?"
  2. "Do you ever think about your energy at a cellular level?"
  3. "Did you know you can now test your mitochondrial function from home — with a few drops of blood?"
  4. "What would it change for you to know your cellular energy score?"

This is not a generic street interview. The interviewer is the differentiator. When someone on the street asks "Wait, how does that actually work?" — I can answer. That's the difference between content that builds trust and content that gets scrolled past.
