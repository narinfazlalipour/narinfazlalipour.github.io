The Testimonial Problem — Solved

High-ticket clients don't want to be on camera. I get it. Here's how we capture their stories anyway:

  • Voice-note testimonials: Client records a 60-second voice memo. We lay it over B-roll of the product in their home. No face, all feeling. Sounds like a podcast clip — feels premium.
  • The Client Wall: "James, Miami — 'I haven't missed a morning plunge in 4 months.'" First name, city, one quote. Anonymous but personal. Stack 6 of these in a carousel.
  • "What 12 Clients Told Us This Month": Aggregate feedback as data. No individuals exposed. Powerful social proof in numbers.
