🟢 What's Working

Strength 1

Product imagery is genuinely premium

The Opus Spa and sauna photography is clean, aspirational, and consistent. Every product shot communicates luxury without trying too hard. The Howie Mandel pin is smart social proof — a celebrity who actually uses the product, not a paid endorsement.

Strength 2

Highlights are well-organized

11 Highlights covering Coldplunges, Saunas, Spas, Installs, Reviews, FAQ, and How-To's. A new visitor can understand the full product line and see real installs in 30 seconds. Most brands in this space have 0-3 Highlights — this is a structural advantage.

🔴 What's Missing

Gap 1

The feed is a product catalog — not a wellness brand

Nearly every post shows a product. There's no content about the wellness lifestyle the products enable — no cold plunge routines, no sauna protocols, no contrast therapy education. The brand sells transformation, but the feed only shows hardware.

Gap 2

Zero founder or customer voice

No founder story, no customer testimonials in-feed, no behind-the-scenes. For a luxury wellness brand, the person behind the vision and the people using the products are the most powerful content assets — and neither appears on the grid.

Diagnosis: Casa Blui has a premium product and a verified badge, but the feed reads like a showroom — not a community. The fastest path to higher engagement is putting people (founder, customers, install teams) into the content alongside the products.

⚡ Three Moves

1

Launch a weekly wellness ritual Reel

One Reel per week showing an actual routine: "My Sunday contrast therapy protocol" or "How I use the cold plunge after a workout." No product push — just lifestyle. Shoot it on an iPhone. The products sell themselves when they're shown in real use.

2

Turn one install into a mini-documentary

Pick one recent install. Film the unboxing, the setup, the first use, and the homeowner's reaction. Cut it into a 3-part carousel or a 60-second Reel. One great install story does more for conversion than 20 product photos.

3

Activate the founder's voice

One post per week from the founder — why they started Casa Blui, what they believe about wellness, a mistake they made building the company. Founder-led content consistently outperforms product-only content for luxury brands. The story behind the spa matters as much as the spa itself.
